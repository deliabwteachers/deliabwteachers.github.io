Mr. Chow, Sing Yin

Mr. Chow is the Panel Chairman of Integrated and Combined Science in school. Mr. Chow strongly believes that mutual understanding and communication between teacher and student are necessary for effective teaching as well as students learning. In order to achieve his goal he had completed the course “Certificate Programme in Student Guidance” organized by the Chinese University of Hong Kong. As social hygiene becomes more important in Hong Kong once after SARS, Mr. Chow enhance his health promoting skill and related knowledge through his study in the course Professional Diploma in Health Education and Health Promotion which organized by the Chinese University of Hong Kong.

Mr. Lo, Ka Fai

Mr.Lo is the panel head of Physics. After graduating with a bachelor’s degree in Science, he further pursued his studies by completing his master’s degree and a postgraduate diploma in Education from the University of Hong Kong. He is passionate about teaching physics and science to his students, wishing them to enjoy the application of the knowledge they acquire from lessons into their everyday life. Realizing the importance of holistic education, he has also joined the Form Management Committee and served as the Form Master who develops pastoral care strategies for students. As an experienced executive member of the Form Management Committee, he assists all Class Teachers in different Forms in handling any issues concerning students’ growth

Mr. Razi, Haris

Mr. Haris teaches Chemistry and Integrated Science. He graduated from the University of Hong Kong (HKU), majoring in Chemistry and Education. Returning to his alma mater has been a refreshing experience for Mr. Haris. Teaching subjects and organizing activities related to STEAM in general, Mr. Haris aims to adopt an interactive learning approach for students so they could understand and appreciate science better. A positive learning environment and the incorporation of technology is what you will find in his classroom! Mr. Haris understands that science could be a difficult subject, but when students feel supported to overcome the hurdles that seem to be unattainable, this will give them confidence and motivation to learn!

Mr. Wan, Ho Yueng

After I graduated from the university, I started my teaching career immediately in a local secondary school. However, after few years of teaching, I wanted to enrich my life experience so I quit my teaching job and started to work in the commercial field. Also, I found that I love travelling so much so I spent another two and a half years for travelling around the world to fulfil my dream. After travelling to around 60 countries in Asia, Europe and the Middle East, I am so satisfied with what I did in the previous years. Now, I am teaching in Broadway and I enjoy meeting people from different cultural background.

Ms. Tahira, Khan

Ms Tahira graduated from Broadway. She studied at The University of Hong Kong majoring in Molecular Biology and Biotechnology. She teaches Mathematics and Biology. Her passion for teaching started when she used to come to Broadway to assist the students in Mathematics tutorials. It gave her a sense of accomplishment whenever the students were able to grasp a concept with her help. Students often find subjects like Mathematics and Science very challenging and give up easily. She hopes to motivate the students to not give up and do their best in the face of challenges.

Ms. Fu, Yuen Yee

Since studying Biology from High School and HKUST, Ms. Fu found that life is interesting and gain large sense of successfulness when she can solve the question with what she learnt. And she hopes that her students can feel the same ways. Biology is a study of life, Education is an influence of life. When these two come together, it is challenging but fruitful to her. When her students fall and stand right back up again, she feels proud of them to be willing to work hard and finally have improvement.
