Mr. Chan, Kwok Keung

Mr Chan Kwok Keung is a music teacher in Delia Memorial School (Broadway). He is well trained and qualified in different aspects of music, majors in music performance, of which he has extensive experience in organizing, managing, scoring, harmonizing, arranging and performing live solo, group and ensemble performances. Mr Chan has been a music technician and composer since 2004. He has extensive experience using recording studios to produce computerized music. Mr Chan’s mode of music teaching emphasized on addressing individual students’ musical interest and adaptive to their individual level of musical skills therefore, allowing individual to develop their interests in the different genre of music and the various aspects of music.

Ms. Ho, Po King

Graduated from Bachelor of Education (Honours) (Visual Arts), minor in Creativity (STEM), in EDUHK and educated in National Taiwan University of Arts in exchange program, Ms Ho was overwhelmed with countless inspiration from different artists as well as culture around the world. Combining with her teaching experiences in Hong Kong and Australia, she wishes to help Broadway students to dream big and get out of the box. “Creativity takes courage”- Henri Matisse. She hopes to be student’s courage and help everyone create their own unique, innovative and expressive artworks.

Ms. Lau, Shut Wai

Ms Lau believe art emphasizes on enjoyment, explorement and experience that allows students to enjoy the fun of art creation and stimulates the enthusiasm for exploring their potential. Ms Lau love teaching Visual Arts and will continue to strive to be a positive influence in their lives.
