Ms. Pong, Fung Wan

After graduating from PolyU with a Bachelor's degree in Language Studies for the Professions (Translation), Ms. Pong joined the teaching force of Broadway, mainly teaching Chinese subjects. To many foreigners, Chinese is one of the most difficult languages in the world.
