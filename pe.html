Mr. Wong, Chun Man

Mr. Wong Chun Man serves as the ECA Master and Subject Panel Head of Physical Education in Broadway. He graduated from the Hong Kong Education University, majoring in Physical Education. Mr. Wong’s athletic accomplishments include being a member of the Hong Kong Judo and Woodball Teams, representing Hong Kong in international competitions, and serving as director of the Hong Kong International Judo Tournament and also the committee member of Tai Po District Councils to the working group on Youth Project. Mr. Wong believes that every student has their own talent. He would like to help students develop their own interests and strengths. Moreover, he also wants to build up their confidence through sports activities.

Mr. Li, Ka Chun

Mr. Li graduated from Education University of Hong Kong (Former Hong Kong Institute of Education) with University degree. He strives to make learning exciting and interesting. His main goal is to motivate students to stretch their own personal limits. His slogan is “I like to run, run makes me smile, I like to run for another mile.” “Do you want to win, I love Broadway.”

Mr. Luk, Ngai Shing

Mr. Luk believes that a whole person’s development is greatly depending on a well-balanced education. Acquiring knowledge and seeking truth from fact are always of the first priority in his teaching mission. Besides, school life plays a critical role for the evolvement of one’s good characters. Mr. Luk strives students’ cooperation as well as taking responsibilities. Students are inculcated to be a person of integrity, discipline, devotion, industriousness, courage, and gratitude through extracurricular activities such as sports, voluntary work and uniform teams. He is keen on taking these as the main means for students to cultivate self-respect and self-esteem.
