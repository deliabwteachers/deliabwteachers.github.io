Ms. Hung, Pik Sai

Ms Hung graduated from the University of Hong Kong with a Bachelor Degree of Arts, majoring in History and majoring in Economics in her Post-graduate Diploma in Education. She has taught different subjects such as History, EPA, Liberal Studies, BET and Economics since she joined the Delia Group of School in 1991. Now she is mainly teaches BET and Economics. Ms Hung believes that education should equip the students to grow into a well-rounded, responsible adult. Therefore, she would like to teach students how to behave properly in their daily life and how to develop good interpersonal skills so that the students will be empowered with not just knowledge and skills, but also good personality to succeed in the future.

Ms. Kwong, Ka Yan

Ms. Kwong was educated at the University of Hong Kong (majoring in Economics and Finance). She received her Master degree from the Hong Kong Polytechnic University, majoring in Accountancy. She obtained a Postgraduate Certificate in Education from the University of Hong Kong before becoming a teacher. After joining Delia Broadway, Ms. Kwong has taught subjects like Economics and BAFS. As a convert to economics, she is particularly attracted to the power of economic theories to offer insights into ostensibly non-economic issues. Ms. Kwong believes that everyone is capable of learning business-related subjects, but a student's desire to learn is a vital pre-requisite to effectively master the concepts and skills. Apart from teaching, Ms Kwong assists school in handling administrative affairs. She is also eager to accompany students in life planning and help them explore various opportunities. During Ms. Kwong's leisure time, she enjoys reading, jogging and hanging out with her friends.
