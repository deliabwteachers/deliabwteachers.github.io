Ms. Chung Shuk Han

Ms. Chung Shuk Han, Valerie is a school social worker of Delia Memorial School (Broadway). She graduated from The Hong Kong Polytechnic University and she has been working in youth work for almost ten years. She comes from Hong Kong Children and Youth Services (NGO). In order to meet our students’ needs on their personal growth, Ms. Chung provides counseling, guidance and consultation services to our students in school. In addition, she organizes various of activities for students to enrich their other learning experiences (OLE). Ms. Chung also conducts preventive, developmental and educational programs such as sex education, drug abuse prevention programs, mental health workshops, developmental trainings, voluntary services to our students. Ms. Chung believes that developing and establishing a heathy and favorable growing environment is crucial to our students. At the same time, the support from both school and family is also significant to strengthen students’ ability to overcome the difficulties and challenges throughout their life. p>
