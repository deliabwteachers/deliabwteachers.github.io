Mr. Ko, Chi Wing

Mr. Ko graduated with a Bachelor Degree of Social Science, majoring in Geography. He focuses on physical geography study and environmental pollution study. Mr. Ko has been teaching in Broadway since 2016. He is the subject chairperson of geography and senior geography teacher. At the same time, he has been senior form class teacher for years. He organized a series of field studies to country parks. He hope that students can learn to appreciate the mother nature and enjoy the beauty of the earth.
