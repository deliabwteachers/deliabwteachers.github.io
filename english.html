ENGLISH TEACHERS

Main Content

Ms. Aniana, Eryn Atienza

Ms. Eryn graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Linguistics and Translation (1st Class Honours) from City University of Hong Kong, and finished her Postgraduate Diploma in Education (English) at the University of Hong Kong. As the Assistant Panel of the English Department, she enjoys finding opportunities for students to further cultivate their English language proficiency, and has mentored outstanding writers for a variety of local competitions. Being a certified Level 1 Google Educator and Apple Teacher, she also advocates for implementing e-learning to engage learners, and is well-versed in leveraging student data to tailor her curriculum and cater for learner diversity. She hopes to continue creating experiences for students to love English, both inside and outside the classroom.

Ms. Camaclang Arian

Ms. Arian obtained her Bachelor’s Degree in English Studies from City University of Hong Kong. During her final year of university, she went to Singapore for an internship at a PR firm and completed a semester abroad in America at San Francisco State University. Prior to joining Delia Broadway, she taught English to kindergarten and primary students for two years. Sharing similar backgrounds with her students, she hopes to be the inspiration for them to be able to achieve anything they set their mind to. Not only does she aim to make learning fun, but Ms. Arian also strives her best to create a safe, positive and an encouraging classroom to unleash her students’ potential. .

Mr. Chan Pak Kan

Having the belief that learning relies greatly on life experience, Mr. Chan aims to engage his students both in and out of the classroom so that they can find places in their lives where the concepts in books are real and apparent. In terms of curriculum design, course content and pedagogy, Mr. Chan believes students are the center and that their varied needs and abilities should be well catered for. Quoting from Rabi Hanina: "I have learned much from my teachers, more from my colleagues, and most from my students.", he considers students to be the main source of inspiration for his teaching and they have so much more to learn from.

Ms. Kaur Gurwinder

Ms Gurwinder has a long-standing relationship with Broadway; she completed all 7 years of her secondary education here and has now been teaching at the school for 6 years. Apart from teaching English, she also manages the affairs of Harmony (one of the four houses of our school) and the student committee as its supervisor. As an assistant form mistress (S.3) and a member of the school’s discipline committee, she helps to guide and monitor students to meet higher standards of academic success, discipline and cooperation amongst one another. Ms. Gurwinder truly believes that no child should be left behind and strives to incorporate that ideology in the execution of all her duties as a teacher, and an alumna, at Broadway.

Mr. Tse Chung Ho

When Mr. Tse started attending high school, he found his drawn to his class teachers because of how fluent he speaks in English and how well-educated he is. He liked to help his teacher before and after school. He knew right then that he would one day become a teacher. From that day forward, he would spend whatever time to be the perfect student in his teacher’s eyes. Mr. Tse is still a rookie in terms of teaching and working in a school. He has been enjoying his time here at Delia, and he met a lot of passionate teachers. Mr. Tse is thrilled to be a part of this big family and he hopes to learn much from all the people here.

Ms. Wong, Siu Ying

Ms. Wong Siu Ying is an experienced English teacher who has taught at Delia group for more than 30 years. She enjoys promoting the joys of learning English in a multifaceted perspective, from action packed English Corner activities engaging students and staff alike to the more humble but yet wildly imaginative creative writing. For Ms. Wong, a love of the subject and a commitment to challenging and enriching students’ learning is what teaching is all about. She hopes that she can share her passion and care for teaching with students and staff alike at Broadway.
