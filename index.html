school principle, mr. lo ka chi

Mr. Lo has been a Principal in Delia Group of Schools since 2001. Before his appointment to this current position in 2011, he had served as the Principal of Matteo Ricci College, Kowloon and Delia Memorial School (Glee Path). Mr. Lo joined Delia Group of Schools as a teacher in 1982. He taught Science, Mathematics and Computer Studies. He had been appointed as School Administrator since 1992. He took up different functional posts including Discipline Master, Prefect of Studies, Assistant Principal and Deputy Principal. He works collaboratively with school administrators, teachers and non-teaching staff to strengthen human resources, upgrade school facilities, reform school curriculum and cultivate the quality school culture. Nurturing Broadwayers to be responsible young citizens and life-long learners have been his educational goals.
